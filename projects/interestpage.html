Background

I love to play volleyball! I have played since I was young and it was always something that was fun for me. Throughout my years of playing I have played lots of different positions, so I think I have a well rounded set of skills on how to play overall.

High school

In High School, I played all 4 years on varisty. My whole time I was a setter. I would say setting is definetly my favorite position to play. I love the connection of how you rely on the passers and then give it to the hitters. I think its fun to be the middle of whats going on. Besides that in school, I was able to be named one of the caption of the teams as well. It was one of the best expirences for me because it allowed me to grow in so many ways and helped guide me to the person I am today.
