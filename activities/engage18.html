What goes in the header?

The title of the page, any thing that will stay at the top of your site.

What goes in the Nav?

In the nav, is the links to the other places in the webpage, it can go in the header, but it doesn't have to. Links in this, stay linked to other places in this same website.

What goes in the main?

Any of the content you want in any of the pages of the webpage.

What goes in the footer?

This can be any last info, and this is where we typically put the copyright ©
