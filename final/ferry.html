Main Ferry Accomidations

As you make plans to travel to the island, one of the first things you should do is look into the ferry schedule and figure out what times and days work best to come to the island. After that you need to make a reservation, you don't have to but to get the best expirence you really should make a reservation.

Coming to the Island

In order to make your reservation you have to pick what time you want to come over. Here is a list of the ferry times Monday-Thursday! It's very hard to get over on the week ends so for the best expirence its best to come on one of these week days.

Making a reservation

Now once you've picked the times and days your going to come and go from the island you need to make a reservation! There are a few things to keep in mind.

  • Reservations fill up quickly, they open up at specific times; at 7 am 2 months before, 2 weeks before and sometimes 2 days before. Make sure to set a reminder and log in at 7 am on the dot!
  • Reservations themself don't cost but if you make a reservation but don't use it you will be fined for that.
  • Once you've made a reservation you have to be 30 minutes early! Make sure to include time to get through the toll both!
  • Lastly there is a cost once you get there, it will depend on the size of your vehicle, and how many people you are traveling with.
